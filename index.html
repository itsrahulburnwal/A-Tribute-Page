A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

(1931-2015)

"All Birds find shelter during rain. But
Eagle avoids rain by flying above clouds." 

Biography:


A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, in full Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam,
(born October 15,1931, Rameswaram, India - died July 27, 2015, Shillong),
Indian scientist and politician who played a leading role in the development of
India's missile and nuclear weapons programs. He was president of India from 2002 to 2007.
